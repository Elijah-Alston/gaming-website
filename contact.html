Wordzies aims to give teenagers a chance to play a word puzzle game and learn three different languages. Our primary goal is to have teens compete against each other to be at the top of the leaderboard to see who is the most fluent in learning a new language. The three languages the teens would be able to choose from are Japanese, Spanish, and French. You can also find us on our Linkedin page @ Wordzies_Linkedin.
